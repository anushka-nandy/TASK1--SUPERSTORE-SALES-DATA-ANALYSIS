SuperStores Sales Data Analysis
->>> Project Overview

This project analyzes a retail/superstore dataset to identify sales trends, regional performance, customer behavior, and profitability drivers using Excel, SQL (PostgreSQL), and Power BI.

The objective is to move beyond basic reporting and deliver actionable business insights that support strategic decision-making.

->>> Tools Used
Excel → Data cleaning, preprocessing, and pivot analysis
SQL (PostgreSQL) → Data extraction, aggregation, and trend analysis
Power BI → Interactive dashboard and data visualization

->>> Key Insights
West region generated the highest sales, indicating strong market demand
Technology category contributed the most to overall profit
Higher discount levels are associated with reduced profitability, especially in certain products
Sales show a declining monthly trend, highlighting potential demand or seasonal issues
A small group of customers contributes disproportionately to total sales (high-value customers)

->>> Business Recommendations
Reduce excessive discounting, especially where it leads to negative or low profit margins
Focus marketing and inventory efforts on high-performing categories (Technology)
Improve performance in underperforming regions (e.g., South) through targeted campaigns
Introduce customer retention strategies for high-value customers
Monitor monthly trends and implement seasonal or promotional strategies to stabilize sales
📂 Project Files
📁 Data → Cleaned dataset & Pivot Tables (Excel)

->>>Conclusion

This project demonstrates an end-to-end data analytics workflow—from data cleaning and transformation to visualization and business insight generation.

It highlights the importance of not just analyzing data, but translating it into actionable strategies that can improve business performance.
